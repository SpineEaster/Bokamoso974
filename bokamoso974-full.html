CORPORATE PROFILE

Lesotho's leading commercial radio station — connecting brands to influential audiences nationwide.

Bokamoso974 is a leading commercial radio station headquartered in Maseru, broadcasting across three key frequencies: 97.4 FM (Maseru & Berea), 98.2 FM (Butha-Buthe, Leribe & Mokhotlong), and 92.6 FM (Mohale's Hoek, Mafeteng & Quthing). With national reach and strong regional penetration, Bokamoso974 connects brands to influential audiences across Lesotho.

Our station is strategically positioned as a premium urban broadcaster, delivering progressive, high-quality programming tailored to a sophisticated and economically active audience. We primarily target listeners aged 24–49, within LSM 4–8, representing emerging and established professionals, entrepreneurs, and decision-makers.

Bokamoso974 is a trusted partner to the corporate sector, providing integrated marketing solutions and impactful advertising campaigns designed to deliver measurable results. Our commercial offerings include brand activations, sponsored content, on-air promotions, digital amplification, and customized campaign strategies aligned with client objectives.

Our programming is structured, dynamic, and informative. Comprehensive news bulletins air at the top of every hour, complemented by dedicated sports, business, and entertainment segments throughout the day to ensure our audience remains informed, engaged, and connected.

Recognized as one of the fastest-growing radio stations in Lesotho, Bokamoso974 operates with a forward-thinking programming philosophy and a modern, data-driven business approach. We are also committed to advancing Lesotho's creative economy by proudly promoting local talent across music, arts, and culture.
